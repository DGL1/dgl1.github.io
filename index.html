Welcome! This is the course page for graduate studies in moral philosophy (G019), a graduate level study of important recent work in ethical theory. Topics and texts may differ year to year. The focus this term is R. Jay Wallace’s manuscript The Moral Nexus (ms).

Requirements

  1. Participants will give a presentation to the seminar. Presentations need only describe the main line of argument of the assigned reading and raise a question or two for discussion.

  2. Those who are enrolled will write a substantial essay of 4500 words.
